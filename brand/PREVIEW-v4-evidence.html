2 · The patterns — what the category actually does

Logo architecture
50% wordmark-primary + simple symbol (JPMorgan, Fidelity, Wise, Nexo, Robinhood). 20% wordmark-only (BlackRock, Vanguard). Almost no symbol-primary.
Typography 2024–2026
Serifs returning for institutional (JPMorgan 2024). Geist is the new standard for contemporary fintech (Belvo 2026 adopted it, Plaid uses it). Custom-drawn wordmarks universal.
Palette formula
Sober base (2 neutrals) + ONE signature color. Zero brand uses 3+ colors as equals. Signatures range: bronze, emerald, crimson, neon green, electric blue.
Symbols (when present)
Single geometric form · Semantic meaning · Favicon-friendly. Pyramid, octagon, flag, cross-hatch, spiral. Never literal metaphors.
Tone 2024–2026 direction
Professional-sophisticated with one "pop" element (Robinhood neon, JPMorgan bronze, Nexo flow). Maturity over playfulness. Heritage-craft (Plaid guilloche) bridged with digital modernity.
