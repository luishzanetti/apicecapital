## 7. App Privacy summary

Per Apple's App Privacy Nutrition Label requirements, the following categories apply:

### Data Linked to You (required disclosure)
- **Financial Info** — Account balances, transaction history, DCA plan parameters — required for core product
- **Identifiers** — User ID, Device ID — authentication and fraud prevention
- **Contact Info** — Email address — account creation, support
- **Usage Data** — Product interaction, lesson progress — Academy experience personalization
- **Diagnostics** — Crash logs, performance data — via Sentry / equivalent

### Data Not Linked to You
- **Analytics** — Aggregate product metrics via PostHog (per V2-MASTER-PLAN Semana 6) — not linked to user ID in aggregate reporting

### Data NOT Collected
- **Health & Fitness** — no
- **Precise Location** — no (only coarse for regulatory-region inference)
- **Contacts** (address book) — no
- **Photos or Videos** — no (except explicit user uploads for support tickets)

## 8. Pricing tension — flagged to Jarvis

**Observed tension:**

| Tier | Product pricing (V2-MASTER-PLAN) | App Store listing above | Delta |
|---|---|---|---|
| Pro | $49.90/mo | $4.99/mo | 10× |
| Club | $149.90/mo | $14.99/mo | 10× |

**Three resolution paths:**

### Path A — Ship listing at product pricing ($49.90 / $149.90)
- ✅ Consistent with web, honest
- ✅ Premium positioning reinforced by price
- ❌ Listing discoverability drops — App Store users skim subscription descriptors; $49.90 reads as "high-ticket" and shrinks top-of-funnel
- ❌ Conversion rates in Finance category fall off cliff above ~$20/mo consumer subscriptions
- Expected install CVR impact: −25 to −40%

### Path B — App Store-specific tiers ($4.99 / $14.99)
- ✅ Maximizes App Store conversion
- ✅ Matches Acorns/Robinhood Gold expected pricing
- ❌ Requires product tier restructure (features at $4.99 cannot be the same as $49.90 web)
- ❌ Potential user confusion if same user sees web pricing + app pricing
- ❌ Revenue per paying user drops — requires 10× volume to compensate (achievable at ASO scale, not at launch)

### Path C — Hybrid (listing Copy in this file: $4.99/$14.99 listed as "Starter" / "Pro-lite" with web-gated full Pro/Club)
- ✅ Honest — both tiers exist
- ✅ Upsell path: app users who want full Pro/Club are pushed to web
- ❌ Confuses the message; adds a fourth tier to a 3-tier product
- Partially validated by competitors: Acorns does this; Betterment does not

**Atlas recommendation to Jarvis → CEO:**
Take Path B for the App Store-specific tiers but **restructure the product around it**: make $4.99 Pro the "mobile-only" tier with 4 of 6 AI experts + 3 of 7 tracks + basic DCA; make $14.99 Club the "mobile-only + ALTIS preview"; reserve $49.90 and $149.90 as WEB-ONLY power tiers for advanced analytics, all 7 tracks, Founder office hours, and export features.

This mirrors how Masterclass, Bloomberg Anywhere, and Wealthfront Premium segment mobile-vs-web. **CEO call required.**

Listing copy above is written on the assumption Path B is approved. If CEO chooses Path A, replace prices in §4 directly — the rest of the copy holds.
