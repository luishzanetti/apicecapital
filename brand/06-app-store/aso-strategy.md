## 4. Category strategy

### Apple App Store — iOS

**Primary category:** `Finance`
**Secondary category:** `Education`

**Rationale:**
- `Finance` is the mandatory home for any app moving money. Robinhood, Coinbase, Wealthfront, Acorns, Cash App all live here. We must compete here.
- `Education` as secondary unlocks:
  - Discovery via the Education tab
  - Apple editorial interest (Apple actively features "Learn" apps)
  - Distinct ranking pool (much less crowded than Finance)
- **Do NOT choose `Business` or `Lifestyle`** — both hurt relevance scoring for financial intent searches.

**Note on "Finance > Crypto":** Apple does not expose a public "Crypto" sub-category. Discovery for crypto-specific users comes from keywords, not category navigation. Positioning as "Finance + Education" is correct.

### Google Play

**Primary category:** `Finance`
**Secondary tags:** `Education`, `Investing` (Google Play allows multiple)

**Rationale:** Google Play is more permissive with multi-tagging. Claim all three.

## 5. Launch tactics

### 5.1 Soft launch — country rollout order

**Phase 1 (Week 1–2) — Hard launch: United States**
- Highest revenue potential
- English primary
- Benchmark against Robinhood, Cash App, Coinbase in native market
- Risk: highest scrutiny from Apple editorial; must ship with polish

**Phase 2 (Week 3–4) — Soft launch expansion: English markets**
- United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Ireland, New Zealand
- Reuse en-US copy; no additional localization cost
- Signal: if CVR holds above 35% in these markets, proceed to Phase 3

**Phase 3 (Week 5–8) — Latin America (Spanish)**
- Mexico, Colombia, Argentina (phased — starting Mexico as highest iOS penetration in LATAM)
- Use `listing-copy-ES.md` LATAM variant
- Different regulatory disclaimer requirements per country (legal review required per market)

**Phase 4 (Month 3+) — España**
- Only if LATAM shows ≥15% share of ES installs
- Swap `portafolio → cartera`, `$ → €`, adjust disclaimers

**Phase 5 (Month 6+) — Opportunistic**
- Brazil (PT-BR) — explicitly deprioritized per feedback_apice_no_localization.md
- Do NOT ship PT-BR listing at launch. CEO directive.

### 5.2 Review/rating strategy

**Launch target:** ≥50 five-star reviews within Week 1 of US public launch

**Tactics:**
1. **In-app review prompt (Apple SKStoreReviewController) after positive events:**
   - After user completes Foundation track L8 (not too early)
   - After user activates their first DCA plan (activation moment)
   - After user earns Level 3 (Navigator) — earned signal
2. **Invite-based launch for ratings seed (Week 0):**
   - CEO's network, beta users, early waitlist
   - Target: 25–30 authentic reviews before public launch
   - Cannot incentivize reviews (Apple TOS violation) — but can remind beta users that launch day is coming
3. **Weekly review monitoring:**
   - Flag 1–2 star reviews within 24 hours
   - CEO or @pm responds publicly via App Store Connect (dev response)
   - Privately via support@ when user left contact

**Target rating trajectory:**
- Launch day: 4.8★ (seeded)
- Month 1: 4.7★ (natural mix)
- Month 3: 4.6★ (steady state — realistic, matches Robinhood 4.3★ / Coinbase 4.6★ range)

### 5.3 App Store featuring pitch

Apple editorial features top apps weekly (Today tab, App of the Day, category collections). Apice should pitch for featuring in month 2–3 (not month 1 — need initial traction metrics).

**Criteria Apple looks for (per developer relations guidance):**

| Criterion | Apice position |
|---|---|
| **Craft in design** | Strong — premium system, V3.01 logo, 65/35 positioning is editorially distinctive |
| **Novel use of platform tech** | Medium — 6 AI Experts is novel; WidgetKit, Live Activities, App Intents (roadmap) |
| **Meaningful category** | Strong — Finance + Education narrative |
| **Story for editors** | Strong — "built to teach, not to trade" is editorial gold |
| **Accessibility support** | REQUIRED to ship at launch — VoiceOver, Larger Text, Dark Interface, Differentiate Without Color, Reduced Motion |
| **Privacy-forward** | Strong — no ATT, minimal data, clean nutrition label |
| **Reviews ≥4.5** | Required; seed toward this |

**Pitch artifacts needed by month 2:**
- 60-second app preview video (add to listing in month 2 — not launch)
- Press kit at `apice.capital/press` (logos, screenshots, founder bio, manifesto excerpt)
- Editorial note (400–500 words) on "why this matters now" — drafted by @copy-chief

### 5.4 Paid ASO support (optional lever)

**Apple Search Ads (ASA) — recommended for launch week only**
- Budget: $500–$2,000/week for first 2 weeks
- Target keywords: ONLY Tier 3 long-tail (high intent, low competition, low CPC: $0.50–$2.00)
- Key terms: `weekly investing`, `bitcoin dca`, `dollar cost averaging app`, `investing academy`
- Do NOT bid on competitor names in Week 1 (wastes budget, invites retaliatory bids)
- Goal: seed install velocity → Apple algorithm rewards trending apps
- **Not a long-term channel — ASA is an ignition spark, not the engine**

## 6. Measurement — ASO KPIs

### Weekly dashboard (PostHog + App Store Connect Analytics)

| KPI | Definition | Launch target | Month 3 target | Month 6 target |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Impressions (search)** | Users who saw Apice in search results | 5K/week | 25K/week | 100K/week |
| **Impressions (browse)** | Users who saw Apice via category browse | 2K/week | 15K/week | 60K/week |
| **Product Page views** | Unique users opening the listing | 1K/week | 10K/week | 50K/week |
| **Taps (search → PP)** | Tap-through from search result | 15% | 20% | 25% |
| **Conversion rate (PP → install)** | Visitors who tapped "Get" | 30% | 35% | 40% |
| **Category rank (Finance, US)** | App's rank in Finance | Top 300 | Top 100 | Top 50 |
| **Keyword rank: "dca"** | Rank for primary keyword | Top 20 | Top 10 | Top 5 |
| **Keyword rank: "weekly investing"** | Rank for white-space keyword | Top 10 | Top 3 | #1 |
| **Keyword rank: "ai investing"** | Rank for Public.com collision | Top 50 | Top 20 | Top 10 |
| **Rating** | Average App Store rating | 4.8★ (seeded) | 4.6★ | 4.6★ |
| **Review velocity** | New reviews per week | 10 | 50 | 150 |

### Monthly deep-dive metrics

- **Screenshot CVR by slot** — which screen drives the most opens? (Apple's App Analytics Creative Sets testing)
- **Keyword ranking movements** — any Tier 1 keywords slipping out of top 20?
- **Competitor position shifts** — is Robinhood/Coinbase shipping new copy we should analyze?
- **Search term discovery** — what unexpected keywords are users searching when they find us?
- **Localization performance** — ES-LATAM CVR vs. EN-US — if <80% of EN CVR, revisit copy
- **"What's New" impact** — does each release's indexed caption produce a 5–10% keyword lift? (Betterment effect)

### Alerts (set in App Store Connect + PostHog)

- Any Tier 1 keyword drops below top 30 → immediate audit
- Rating drops below 4.5★ → crisis protocol (CEO + @pm + @qa review)
- Product Page CVR drops below 30% for 2 consecutive weeks → A/B test new first screenshot
- Category rank drops out of top 300 → review if Apple made an algorithm change
