# Apice Capital — Weekly Content System

> **Owner:** @media-head
> **Status:** v1 FINAL — 2026-04-17
> **Rhythm:** 7 days, 7 voices, 1 weekly newsletter anchor
> **Voices:** Each day belongs to one of The Six AI Experts (per D4 decision) + a Sunday manifesto slot.

---

## 0. Why this system exists

Apice's core thesis — *"Build Wealth. One Week at a Time."* — must be experienced, not just advertised. The weekly content calendar turns the tagline into a product surface. Every day a member opens the app or an Apice channel, one of The Six greets them with a discipline-specific lens.

This system is the organic growth engine. Paid acquisition brings people in; this rhythm keeps them.

## 2. Day-by-day specification

### MONDAY — Kai · Market Intelligence

**Voice:** Analytical, calm, non-alarmist. Kai reads the week's setup without selling fear or greed.

**Format:** Instagram square post (1080×1080) + LinkedIn post (1200×628) + 3-line in-app morning ritual card.

**Template:** `instagram-post-2.svg` adapted — big stat hero + caption.

**Length:**
- IG caption: 60–120 words
- LinkedIn: 180–250 words
- In-app ritual: 2–3 sentences

**Content formula:**
1. **One macro signal** of the week (rates, Fed, ETF flows, on-chain macro)
2. **One behavioral takeaway** ("The data says X. Your plan says keep going.")
3. **Zero price predictions**

**Sample headline:** *"Fear & Greed: 38. What that actually means."*
**Sample caption:**
> F&G index at 38 is not a signal to buy. It's a reminder that sentiment is always late. Your DCA plan was set when sentiment was 60. That's the point. — Kai

**CTA:** "See this week's brief inside Apice." (link to Market Intelligence track L1 preview)

---

### TUESDAY — Omar · Education Snippet

**Voice:** Patient, encouraging, never condescending. Omar teaches one concept per week.

**Format:** Instagram carousel (5–7 slides, 1080×1080 each) + TikTok :45 micro-lesson (vertical 1080×1920).

**Template:** `instagram-post-3.svg` for the opening slide (quote-grade intro), then slides 2–6 follow the carousel spec below.

**Carousel slide framework:**
- Slide 1 (cover): Question hook *("What is a CAGR, actually?")*
- Slide 2: Plain-English definition
- Slide 3: Why it matters for DCA / long-term
- Slide 4: Worked example with real numbers
- Slide 5: Common misunderstanding
- Slide 6: "Try it in Apice" (link to relevant tool)
- Slide 7 (close): Omar byline + next week teaser

**Length:** each slide ≤ 20 words body; hero headline ≤ 6 words.

**TikTok version:** same concept, 45s spoken POV, text overlay mirrors carousel hook.

**Sample topics (quarter 1):**
- Week 1: What CAGR actually measures
- Week 2: Why "average return" is a liar
- Week 3: Standard deviation, without the math degree
- Week 4: Drawdown — the real enemy

### WEDNESDAY — Elena · DCA Discipline Reminder

**Voice:** Quiet, steady, Zen-adjacent. Elena is the counterbalance to Kai's signal-heavy Monday.

**Format:** Instagram story (1080×1920) + push notification + in-app banner at 8:00 local time.

**Template:** `instagram-story.svg` with streak number swapped + reminder sub.

**Length:**
- Story text: one-liner + caption ≤ 12 words
- Push: ≤ 60 chars ("Your Wednesday is your weekly. ⌛")
- Banner: 1 sentence + CTA

**Content formula:**
- "It's Wednesday. Your DCA runs in {X} days."
- OR: "{N} members executed their plan today. You're not alone."
- OR: Micro-prompt: "Open Apice. Look at your streak. That's the whole post."

**Sample caption:**
> Wednesday is quiet work. The market does what it does. You do what you said you'd do. — Elena

**CTA:** "See your plan" (deep-link to DCA Plan card)

### THURSDAY — Nora · Thesis Spotlight

**Voice:** Conviction-forward, patient-aggressive. Nora builds the long view.

**Format:** LinkedIn long-form post (800–1200 words) + Instagram post summarizing (1080×1080) + X/Twitter thread (7–9 tweets).

**Template:** `linkedin-post.svg` as the header image.

**Content formula:**
1. **One thesis** (not a prediction): e.g., "The next decade of wealth-building looks nothing like the last."
2. **Three supporting evidences** (macro, behavioral, product)
3. **One counter-argument** (Nora acknowledges the other side)
4. **One reader prompt** ("What's your thesis?")

**Length:**
- LinkedIn: 800–1200 words, 3–4 paragraphs
- IG post: 3–4 sentence summary with link-in-bio
- X thread: 7–9 tweets, one idea each, final tweet CTA

**Sample thesis (Q1):**
- "Why $50/week will outperform $5,000/quarter — even if the $5,000 has better timing."
- "The real Bitcoin thesis is a behavioral one, not a monetary one."

### FRIDAY — Maya · Market Close Insights

**Voice:** Researcher, data-first, neutral. Maya wraps the week.

**Format:** **The Apice Weekly newsletter** (primary) + Instagram post + short YouTube (5–7 min).

**Template:** `email-header-hero.svg` for newsletter; `instagram-post-2.svg` for IG.

**Newsletter structure (The Apice Weekly):**
1. **Header:** Issue number + date + a single big-idea headline
2. **The Week in Numbers:** 4 stats (market + on-chain + behavioral + Apice community)
3. **One Idea, Deep:** 400-word essay (Maya's voice)
4. **Behind the Code:** product or content update from Apice team (100 words)
5. **Member Spotlight:** 1 anonymized stat from our user base (with consent) — "Member since January completed their 12th weekly DCA this week."
6. **The Week Ahead:** 3 dates to watch (never "trade setups" — only events)
7. **Apice Code of the Week:** one manifesto line (teaser for Sunday)

**Length:** 900–1400 words, 5–7 min read.

**IG version:** the Stats section exported as a post; newsletter CTA in caption.

**Sample headline (newsletter):**
> Issue 04 · March 14, 2026 — *"The week the Fed blinked, and why your plan didn't care."*

- One application: what that means for this week's portfolio

**Length:** ≤ 80 words total. Saturday is for reflection, not information.

**Sample post:**

> Caption: Saturday reflection. Check your allocation. Not to act. To know. — Dante

**CTA:** "Run your portfolio health check." (deep-link to Portfolio Architecture track L1)

## 5. Reuse + remix rules

- Every weekly piece has 3 reuse paths:
  1. **Same-week cross-channel** — IG → X → LinkedIn with format adaptation
  2. **Quarterly anthology** — Friday newsletters compile into a quarterly PDF digest (lead magnet)
  3. **Ad fuel** — top-performing organic posts get promoted as Archetype A/B/C ads (see `campaign-playbook.md` § 1)
- Carousels (Tuesday) become **TikTok + Reels** within 48h — same script, vertical reshoot.
- Newsletter sections become standalone posts the following week.

## 7. Guardrails (specific to weekly content)

1. **One voice per day.** Never cross the voices. Elena doesn't do market intelligence. Kai doesn't do risk reflection.
2. **No "trade ideas."** Ever. Apice does not publish trades. We publish frameworks and discipline prompts.
3. **No financial predictions** with numeric price targets. Directional theses from Nora are acceptable; "BTC will hit $150k" is not.
4. **No gatekeeping.** If a lesson teaser works organically, the full lesson must be accessible in the free tier. We don't tease and wall simultaneously.
5. **Evergreen where possible.** Wednesday, Saturday, Sunday templates are 80% evergreen — swap one variable (streak number, quote tenet) to ship. Reserve maximum effort for Tuesday (Omar) and Friday (Maya).

## 8. Launch ramp

- **Weeks 1–2:** Fridays only (newsletter + IG). Prove the anchor works.
- **Weeks 3–4:** Add Tuesday (Omar) + Sunday (Apice Code).
- **Weeks 5–6:** Add Monday (Kai) + Thursday (Nora).
- **Week 7+:** Add Wednesday (Elena) + Saturday (Dante) — the full rhythm.

Rationale: start with the highest-leverage days, build production discipline, scale to full 7-day rhythm once the team has 6 weeks of practice.
