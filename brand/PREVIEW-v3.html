Uma's recommendation — CAPITELLO

"Apice is not a place you visit. It is a monument you stand next to every week. A stamp is transactional; a monument is structural. At $149.90/month, the Club tier is not selling a better trading interface — it is selling membership in an institution, and institutions communicate through monumental lettering."

The weight (1.2u stems where competitors run 0.6u hairlines), the size-responsive craft (serif stubs above 96px), the single-primitive system (the 1.2u rule appearing in lockup, nav, certificate, Manifesto) compose a house style that is both contemporary (Linear-grade rigor) and ancient (Trajan-column lineage). It accrues equity rather than decays.

CIFRA is beautiful, and a weaker competitor would ship it and win. CAPITELLO is inevitable, and that is why it is the direction Apice ships.
